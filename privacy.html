We built Taskly to help people do great work — not to monetize their personal data. We will never sell your data to third parties.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated through your use of Taskly.

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash — we never see your actual password).

Profile information: You may optionally add a job title, company name, or other details to your profile.

Usage data: We collect data about how you use Taskly, including tasks created, projects managed, and features used. This helps us improve the product.

Device & technical data: We collect your browser type, IP address, device type, and operating system for security and performance purposes.

Payment information: If you upgrade to a paid plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your full card number — only the last 4 digits and card type for display purposes.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain Taskly
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process payments and send billing-related emails
  • Send you product updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage to improve the product
  • Detect, investigate and prevent fraudulent transactions and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data to serve you third-party advertisements.

3. How we share your information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share your information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: We work with trusted third parties (Supabase for database hosting, Stripe for payments, and similar providers) who process data only on our behalf and under strict data protection agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.
  • Business transfers: If Taskly is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before this happens.
  • With your consent: We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

4. Data storage & security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure, which is hosted on AWS and is SOC 2 compliant. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance reasons.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your Taskly account.

5. Cookies

Taskly uses a small number of essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may prevent Taskly from working correctly.

6. Your rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view and download your data from your Profile settings at any time.
  • Correction: You can update your name, email, and other profile information directly in the app.
  • Deletion: You can request account deletion from your Profile page. We will process this within 30 days.
  • Portability: You can export your task and project data in CSV format from your dashboard.
  • Objection: You may object to certain uses of your data by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, you can use the in-app settings or email us at privacy@taskly.io.

7. Children's privacy

Taskly is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

8. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@taskly.io
  • Contact form: taskly.io/contact

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
